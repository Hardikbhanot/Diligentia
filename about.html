Welcome to Diligentia, The Entrepreneurship Cell of PGDAV College.

The story begins in 2021 when Diligentia was established to enkindle students with the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship and aid them with resources & knowledge to be successful entrepreneurs. The logo of Diligentia - The Mountain epitomises that every major success is built on the solid bedrock of previous failures. Diligentia is committed to complying with the foundation of entrepreneurship through knowledge of startups and bringing together ambitious minds who are never afraid to take risks.
We aim at imparting the values of an entrepreneur through various Workshops, Interactive sessions with Industry Experts, Live Projects, Mentorship Programs and Motivational Talks. Whether you are inclined towards entrepreneurship or have an innovative mind that can trace out solutions creatively, Diligentia is the platform for you.
